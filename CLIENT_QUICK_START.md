# Client Quick Start — Your AI Content Pipeline in 5 Minutes

Welcome! Your SeedLink content automation is live. Here's everything you need to know.

---

## 1. Your Content Hub (Google Sheet)

You have a Google Sheet with 5 tabs. You only need to check 2 regularly:

- **Content Hub** — Your blog posts. Check the **Status** column:
  - `Ready for Review` = New AI draft waiting for your review
  - `Approved` = You approved it; social posts are being generated
  - `Published` = Live on your blog and social channels

- **Social Queue** — LinkedIn and Twitter posts derived from your approved blog content

## 2. How to Review & Approve Content

1. You'll get a Slack/email notification when a new draft is ready
2. Open your Google Sheet → **Content Hub** tab
3. Read the draft in the **Draft Content** column
4. If it looks good: change **Status** to `Approved`
5. If it needs changes: change **Status** to `Needs Manual Review` and add notes in the **Notes** column

That's it. Once approved, social posts are auto-generated and scheduled.

## 3. Your Weekly Rhythm

| Day | What Happens | Your Action |
|-----|-------------|-------------|
| Monday | 5-7 new topics auto-generated | Review topics (optional — they're pre-vetted) |
| Tue–Thu | Blog drafts auto-generated | Review & approve drafts |
| Daily | Approved posts published to social | Nothing — it's automatic |
| Friday | Weekly analytics report | Review performance summary |

## 4. Voice Calibration (First 2 Weeks)

During your first 2 weeks, rate how well the AI captures your voice:

| Score | What It Means | Action |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| 1 | Completely wrong — doesn't sound like me at all | We'll rebuild your voice profile |
| 2 | Wrong direction — tone or style is off | We'll make significant adjustments |
| 3 | Getting there — right direction but needs tuning | We'll refine specific aspects |
| 4 | Close — sounds like me with minor tweaks | One more round of fine-tuning |
| 5 | Nailed it — this sounds exactly like me | Voice profile locked in! |

Share your rating and any specific feedback via Slack or in the Notes column.

## 5. Need Help?

- **Email** — support@seedlink.app for formal requests
- **Emergency** — If something published that shouldn't have, message Slack immediately
